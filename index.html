About

Precision in motion and measure

I work at the intersection of robotics and aerospace-minded systems engineering: mechanisms that must move reliably, estimators that must be correct under uncertainty, and interfaces that must feel deliberate—not accidental. From CHARM Lab soft robots to autonomous tracking and precision mechanical design, I care that every part earns its place.

Detail

GD&T-aware fabrication, repeatable experiments, calibrated instrumentation, and tolerances treated as design requirements—not afterthoughts.

Functionality

Systems judged by what they do: load capacity, loop stability, association accuracy, seal integrity, and data you can trust on the next run.

Elegance

Clean architecture in hardware and software—dual-mesh haptics, minimal moving paths, coherent CAD, and experiences that feel intentional to the user.
